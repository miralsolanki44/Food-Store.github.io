About Impromptu Gourmet

THE ART OF DINING IN

At Impromptu Gourmet, we’ve perfected the art of dining in with chef-inspired recipes that are easy to prepare. Delicious food made simple, these words are our mission. We are proud to offer a convenient assortment of prepared gourmet frozen meals and desserts delivered right to your door. Whether it's a family dinner, a weekend cookout, or a special occasion. We want to make your life better one meal at a time -- to make the ordinary special, and the special extraordinary.

Bon appetit!
